Treating your containers properly

In order to maintain the longevity of your product use these key methods.

Assembly

Your order may require assembly and arrive in pieces along with a link to the construction video. Keep these in mind when building.

  • Your ablity to assemble your box by hand will very by strength
  • The best substitute for hand assembly is a rubber mallet
  • When assembling make sure not to apply excessive force especially to smaller components and living hinges
  • If it feels like your breaking it you probably are
